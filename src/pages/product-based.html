Product Based Email Sendbox

Ideal email type for eCommerce Solutions

Target is SKU

Insert your products with SKU and while sending your emails use only SKU.

Sending the email

All you need is to post placeholder values to API with your product SKU. The system will send the email and log the request.

Auditing and resending the emails

All API calls and emails are saved to the database. If you don't want to save any sensitive variable, you may mark placeholders as sensitive information. Unfortunately, if an email has sensitive data, you can't resend the email. You can always generate and send emails using online form.
